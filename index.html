Blog

Making Things That Last

Feb 2024

Thoughts on sustainable design and the beauty of aging materials...

When we think about design, we often focus on the immediate impact - how something looks, feels, or functions right now. But what about how it ages? How it develops character over time? This is where the true beauty of sustainable design emerges.

In this exploration of lasting design, we'll look at:

  • Materials that age gracefully
  • Design principles for longevity
  • The environmental impact of lasting products

The Art of Concrete

Jan 2024

Exploring the possibilities of concrete in modern furniture design...

Concrete - a material often associated with cold, industrial spaces - has untapped potential in furniture design. Its raw beauty and versatility offer unique opportunities for creating pieces that are both functional and sculptural.

Key aspects we'll explore:

  • Mixing techniques for different finishes
  • Form design and mold making
  • Surface treatments and sealing
