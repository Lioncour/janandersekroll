Privacy Policy for Fylgja

Last Updated:

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fylgja ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

Fylgja does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information.

The app operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Transmit any information to third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Require an internet connection for core functionality

Local Data Storage

Any data created or stored by the app (such as user preferences or app-specific content) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us or any third parties and remains under your control.

Third-Party Services

Fylgja does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. The app does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or any other data collection mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

Since Fylgja does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. No personal information is collected from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over any local data stored on your device, which you can manage through your device settings.
